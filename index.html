Our Team

Ashaa Balasubramaniam Founder

Ashaa Balasubramaniam is born and grew up in Bangalore, has great inclination towards the city’s climate, people, food and fusion culture. She is passionate about doing anything creative, while experimenting with food was always her interest. She, being a foodie, realized the limited availability of healthy options to satisfy one’s cravings, especially in the evenings after long hours of work or study. This thought provoked to cautiously design the menu and make Tummy Fuel a perfect fusion place for health & cravings. Yes, recipes at Tummy Fuel are her brainchild, Ashaa Balasubramaniam personally trains her staff, oversees the preparation and dining experience of every guest with a smile on her face.

Sashidharran Deivasigamani Co-Founder

Sashidharran Deivasigamani is a native of Coimbatore, with education in Apparel and further Masters in Business Management. An enthusiastic cyclist, voracious reader and healthy eating fanatic, he believed in “Fitness is 20% exercise and 80% Nutrition”. Further, Sashidharran allows himself to explore his interest in cooking and hand in hand ensure to create a most sustainable and growing entrepreneurship for both Partners. With his colossal work experience in both India / abroad, Sashidharran blends himself at his best to handle administration, Vendor Partners and financial analytics at Tummy Fuel.
